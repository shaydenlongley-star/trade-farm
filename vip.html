VIP Rewards

Every purchase earns you points. Redeem for discounts, access exclusive member offers and unlock tier benefits the more you spend.

Earn & Redeem
How It Works

The More You Buy, The More You Save

The Trade & Farm VIP Rewards program is free to join and automatically earns you points every time you make a purchase in-store at either Port Vila or Santo. Points accumulate and can be redeemed against future purchases.

As your spending grows, you move through our VIP tiers — unlocking better earn rates, exclusive promotions and member-only pricing on selected product lines.

Member
