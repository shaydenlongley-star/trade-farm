About CG&A

Precision Glass & Aluminium Solutions

Trade & Farm's Commercial Glass and Aluminium (CG&A) division provides high-quality glazing and aluminium fabrication for builders, architects and property owners across Vanuatu. We supply and install a comprehensive range of products designed for Pacific conditions — built to withstand tropical heat, humidity and cyclone-force winds.

From a single window replacement to full shopfront glazing for a commercial development, our team delivers precise, professional results on every job.

Our Products

Glass & Aluminium Solutions

Aluminium Windows

Powder-coated aluminium window frames in a range of profiles. Single and double glazed options available for thermal and acoustic performance.

Aluminium Doors

Sliding, hinged and bi-fold aluminium door systems — lightweight, low maintenance and suited to Pacific weather.

Shopfronts & Facades

Commercial shopfront systems with glass panels, aluminium framing and security glazing options for retail and office buildings.

Louvres

Aluminium louvre windows and blades — ideal for natural ventilation in tropical climates while maintaining security and weather protection.

Safety & Security Glass

Toughened, laminated and security-rated glass for high-risk locations, cyclone zones and commercial applications.

Custom Fabrication

Custom aluminium sections cut, fabricated and finished to your specifications — balustrades, screens, canopies and bespoke architectural elements.

Our Process

From Quote to Installation

01

Site Visit & Measure

Our CG&A team visits your site, takes precise measurements and assesses the best product options for your project and budget.

02

Quote & Approval

We prepare a detailed written quote covering materials, fabrication and installation. No surprises.

03

Fabrication

Your order is cut and fabricated in our workshop to exact specifications, with the appropriate finish and glazing specified.

04

Professional Installation

Our experienced installation team fits your windows, doors or glazing to manufacturer specifications and industry standards.
