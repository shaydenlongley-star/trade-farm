Who We Are

Your Partner in Building Vanuatu

Trade & Farm has been at the heart of Vanuatu's construction and agricultural sector for decades. We supply everything from individual tradespeople and farmers through to large commercial contractors and government projects.

With deep roots across both Efate and Santo, we understand the local challenges — cyclone resistance, island logistics, sourcing quality products — and we're here to make your job easier.
