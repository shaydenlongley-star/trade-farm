Serving the North

Santo's Hardware Specialist

The Santo store is Trade & Farm's northern branch, serving Luganville and the wider Espiritu Santo island. As the second-largest island in Vanuatu, Santo has a strong agricultural sector and growing construction activity — and our Santo store is stocked to meet that demand.

The same product range, the same service standards, and the same trade account and VIP programs as our Port Vila flagship — available right here in Luganville.
