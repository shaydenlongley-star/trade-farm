What We Stock

Everything for Your Project

The Port Vila store carries the full Trade & Farm product range across all major building and farm supply categories.

Structural Materials

Timber, steel, cement, reinforcing and all the structural elements to build foundations and frames.

Hardware & Tools

Hand tools, power tools, fasteners, fixings and accessories — DeWalt, Stanley, Honda and more.
