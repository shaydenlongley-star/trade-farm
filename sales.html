Dealer Program
What is SDEP?

Grow Your Business With Our Support

The Supplies Dealer Education Program (SDEP) is Trade & Farm's structured dealer program for registered resellers across Vanuatu. It's designed to give small and medium hardware and supply businesses access to the same quality products, pricing advantages and supplier support that larger operators enjoy.

Through SDEP, you gain preferential wholesale pricing, direct access to our product team, training on new product lines, and marketing materials to help you sell more effectively.

Program Benefits

What You Get as an SDEP Dealer

Preferential Pricing

Access wholesale rates not available to retail customers. The more you order, the better your margins.

Priority Stock Access

SDEP dealers are first to access new stock, fast-moving lines and import shipments before they hit the retail floor.

Dedicated Support

A dedicated Trade & Farm account manager is assigned to your business — one point of contact for orders, queries and support.

Business Training

Regular product education sessions and sales training to help your staff sell confidently and correctly.

Brand Materials

Access to approved marketing assets, product datasheets and promotional materials for the brands we stock.

Flexible Orders

Order in the quantities that work for your business — bulk orders, split deliveries, or store pick-up across both locations.

What We Supply

Products Built for The Pacific

As an SDEP dealer, you gain access to our full product catalogue — from structural timber and steel to paints, tools, and farm supplies. All products are selected for suitability in Pacific Island conditions.

  • Timber, cement, steel and structural materials
  • Electrical, plumbing and hardware
  • Power tools from DeWalt, Honda and more
  • Paints and coatings — Dulux, Cabot's, Intergrain
  • Agricultural supplies and farm equipment
